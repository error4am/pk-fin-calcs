Salary Tax Slabs Pakistan 2026

A simple guide to Pakistan salaried income tax slabs for FY 2025-2026, with practical notes to help you understand annual tax and take-home salary.

Quick Overview

If you want to understand how salary tax works in Pakistan, the first thing to know is that the system is progressive. That means different parts of your income are taxed at different rates as your annual salary increases. Employees often hear a slab percentage and assume their entire salary is taxed at that rate, but that is not how the calculation works.

This guide summarizes the salaried slabs used for FY 2025-2026 and links directly to the calculator page so you can move from reading the rule to estimating your own monthly deduction in PKR.

Pakistan Salaried Tax Slabs FY 2025-2026

  • Up to PKR 600,000: 0%
  • PKR 600,001 to 1,200,000: 5% of the amount above PKR 600,000
  • PKR 1,200,001 to 2,400,000: PKR 30,000 + 15% of the amount above PKR 1,200,000
  • PKR 2,400,001 to 3,600,000: PKR 210,000 + 25% of the amount above PKR 2,400,000
  • PKR 3,600,001 to 6,000,000: PKR 510,000 + 30% of the amount above PKR 3,600,000
  • Above PKR 6,000,000: PKR 1,230,000 + 35% of the amount above PKR 6,000,000

How to Read the Slabs

Only the income inside each band is taxed at that band's rate. Because of this, the top slab you enter is not the same thing as your overall effective tax rate. Your effective rate is usually much lower.
